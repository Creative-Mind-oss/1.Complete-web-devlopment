Indian Permier Leauge

The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India, established in 2008 by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI). It is one of the most popular and lucrative cricket leagues in the world, known for its fast-paced matches and star-studded teams.The IPL was the first sporting event in the world to be broadcast live on YouTube (in 2010).
